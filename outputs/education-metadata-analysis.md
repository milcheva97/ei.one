# EI.one Education Page Metadata Analysis

Source target: `https://ei.one/en/education`

## Recommended metadata

- **Title:** `Education - EI.one School Ecosystem & Academy`
- **Meta description:** `EI.one Education connects school ecosystems, academy journeys and verified experiences so learning becomes visible, attestable and useful for future opportunities.`
- **Canonical:** `https://ei.one/en/education`
- **Keywords:** `EI.one education, school ecosystem, academy, experience identity, verified learning, talents, skills`
- **Open Graph type:** `website`
- **Open Graph title:** Same as title.
- **Open Graph description:** Same as meta description.
- **Twitter card:** `summary_large_image`

## Content strategy

The page should make Education feel like one operational part of the EI.one ecosystem, not only a school or course page. The strongest positioning is:

- Schools and academy partners create structured learning paths.
- Learners turn projects, mentoring and internships into verified experience.
- Companies can later discover people through proof of what they actually did.

## Improvements implemented

- Added dynamic React metadata for the Education route.
- Created a dedicated `/education` page route in the app.
- Rebuilt the page around four sections: hero, pillars, academy flow, outcomes, and CTA.
- Used brighter education imagery and EI.one brand colors.
- Added richer internal linking to Academy, Experience Identity, Community and Contact.
